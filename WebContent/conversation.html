Conversation

 

There is an increasing lack of dialog. We tend to quickly form an opinion or draw a conclusion. This leads to unnecessary polarization. By challenging visitors to start a dialog we want to make them aware of the prejudices. We would like to see the quick assumptions turned into an open mind.
