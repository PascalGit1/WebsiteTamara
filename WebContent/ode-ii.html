Ode II

 

I lay my legs

next to the bed

my torso feels dull

and heavy, my ears

only hear

the murmur of my blood

 

My arms lay as

broken wings

on the sheet

the quicksand closes in

above me

my breath suffers

and all the air

is squeezed from my lungs

 

I am sinking

further and further in the

underworld of

a dull sleep

of which I only

awake after centuries
