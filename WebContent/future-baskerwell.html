Future Baskerwell

 

Type classification is used as a guideline to identify, choose and combine typefaces. I used 3 different categories, Serif, Sans serif and Slab serif. We then took 3 typefaces which are representative for their category, the Futura, Baskerville and Rockwell. I took the fonts apart to find out what defines not only the font but also the category the font is in. Now you can design your own new original letter which can be a sans serif, serif, slab serif, or a whole new kind of type.
