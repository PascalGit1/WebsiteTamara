Ode

 

They have been together for over 60 years. They used to have a store together where they sold seeds. He is characteristically calm and matter-of-fact but also a poet. She is humble and always living in service of him, taking care of the children and the household and never longing for more.

When they grew older he got Alzheimer's, she as always, trying to take care of him and the children, grandchildren, cooking, sewing, clothes and cleaning.

They come together again in this book, his poems and her sewing.

 

 

 

If I treated you unreasonably

due to my stubbornness

my moodiness

I humbly ask you

to accept my apologies

because I love you

I love you
